How We Determine Your Car Shipping Rate

Your auto transport quote is based on several important factors: the distance of your shipment, vehicle size and weight, the type of trailer service you select, pickup and delivery locations, the season, fuel costs, and how quickly you need the vehicle delivered.

Enclosed transport, larger vehicles, and shipments to rural or difficult-to-reach areas may require more resources, which can increase the rate. Seasonal demand and fuel price changes can also affect pricing. If you need expedited service, we can arrange it for an additional fee.

Our team is here to walk you through these considerations and help tailor the most cost-effective solution for your needs, without compromising on quality or care.

  • Distance and delivery timeframe
  • Accessibility of pickup and drop-off locations
  • Season or time of year
  • Vehicle size and weight
  • Chosen transport method (open or enclosed)
  • Fuel prices at the time of shipment
